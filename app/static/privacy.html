Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 21, 2025

1. Information We Access

Our Email Spam Detection application accesses your Gmail data solely for the purpose of identifying and managing spam emails. We access:

  • Email sender information
  • Email subject lines
  • Email body content
  • Email metadata (date, ID)

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your Gmail data to:

  • Analyze and classify emails as spam or not spam
  • Move identified spam emails to trash (with your permission)
  • Provide you with downloadable reports of spam detection results

3. Data Storage

We DO NOT store your email data permanently. All email processing is done in-memory and data is automatically cleared after 2 hours of inactivity. We do not save emails to any database or external storage.

4. Data Sharing

We DO NOT:

  • Sell your data to third parties
  • Share your emails with advertisers
  • Use your data for any purpose other than spam detection
  • Store your credentials or access tokens beyond your session

5. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encrypted HTTPS connections
  • Secure OAuth 2.0 authentication
  • Session-based access control
  • Automatic session expiration after 15 minutes of inactivity

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Revoke access to your Gmail account at any time via Google Account Permissions
  • Request deletion of any temporarily cached data
  • Stop using the service at any time
